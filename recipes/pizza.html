Italian Pizza

This is a classic Italian pizza with a crispy crust and flavorful toppings. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 (.25 ounce) envelope active dry yeast
  • 1 cup lukewarm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons shortening
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • ¾ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¼ teaspoon dried marjoram
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Cheese:

  • 1 (12 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Toppings:

  • ½ cup sliced black olives
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup sliced pepperoni

Instructions

Dough:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in lukewarm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, shortening, and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms. Knead on a floured surface until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes. Place in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.

Sauce:

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in tomato paste, water, salt, sugar, oregano, garlic powder, basil, marjoram, cumin, chili powder, red pepper flakes, and black pepper. Simmer until slightly thickened, about 15 minutes.

Assembly:

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C). Punch down dough and roll out on a floured surface to fit a 12-inch pizza pan. Transfer to pizza pan. Spread sauce over dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and arrange toppings on top.
  2. Bake in preheated oven until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly, about 15 minutes. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your Pizza!
