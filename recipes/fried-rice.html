Hibachi-Style Fried Rice

This flavorful hibachi-style fried rice is made with rice, eggs, vegetables, and a savory soy sauce blend. It makes a great side dish or main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups cooked white rice, chilled
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble until just set, then transfer to a plate.
  3. Add the remaining oil and butter to the pan. Sauté garlic and mixed vegetables until tender.
  4. Add the chilled rice and stir-fry until heated through and slightly golden.
  5. Return the cooked eggs to the pan. Stir in soy sauce, sesame oil, and green onions.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, toss to combine, and cook for another minute.
  7. Serve hot with extra green onions or soy sauce if desired.

Enjoy your hibachi-style fried rice!
