Best Sweet and Sour Brisket

This is a simple brisket recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds beef brisket
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Place brisket in a roasting pan. Mix water, ketchup, vinegar, onions, garlic, brown sugar, and salt together in a bowl; pour over brisket.
  3. Cover roasting pan with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven for 3 hours. Remove foil and bake an additional 30 minutes.

Enjoy your brisket!
